## Key Finding: The Trades Chrome Extension Space Is EMPTY

Of ~112,000 Chrome extensions, only 3 target trades contractors directly — and all 3 are garbage (link aggregators with 2.3-star ratings). Zero extensions exist for electricians, HVAC, painters, landscapers, concrete, fencing, or any specific trade.

---

## Top 4 Zafto Funnel Extensions

### 1. Thumbtack/Angi Lead Enhancer
- **Competition**: ZERO extensions exist for any contractor lead platform
- **What it does**: Auto-respond templates, lead scoring/filtering, cost-per-lead analytics, one-click estimate generation from lead details
- **Zafto funnel**: "Export leads to Zafto CRM" as premium feature
- **Revenue potential**: Very high — contractors spend thousands/mo on leads

### 2. Contractor New Tab Dashboard
- **Competition**: ZERO contractor-specific new tab pages
- **What it does**: Today's jobs, weather at each job site, outstanding invoices, lead notifications
- **Zafto funnel**: Branded "Powered by Zafto" + free trial CTA on every new tab
- **Revenue potential**: Free (acquisition channel for Zafto subscriptions)

### 3. "Add to Estimate" on Supply House Sites
- **Competition**: BuildClub does price comparison but NOT estimate integration
- **What it does**: One-click captures item/price/quantity from Home Depot, Lowes, Ferguson, ABC Supply into a Zafto estimate
- **Zafto funnel**: Requires Zafto account — direct conversion tool
- **Revenue potential**: Locks contractors into Zafto estimating workflow

### 4. Home Inspector Property Overlay
- **Competition**: ZERO extensions for home inspectors
- **What it does**: Overlays inspection-relevant data on Zillow/Redfin listings (permit history, system ages, common defects for construction era)
- **Zafto funnel**: Links directly to Zafto inspector workflows
- **Revenue potential**: Unique to Zafto's inspector entity type

## Proven Revenue Benchmark

**RightTool for QuickBooks Online**: 42,000+ users at $75/mo per user = potentially $3.15M/mo from a Chrome extension that just adds shortcuts to QuickBooks. This proves enterprise-adjacent extensions can command premium pricing.
