Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

The short version: CopyUnlock collects zero data. Nothing leaves your device. No analytics, no tracking, no network requests. Your clipboard history is stored locally and never transmitted anywhere.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CopyUnlock ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles information when you use our Chrome browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

CopyUnlock is published by PeakTools Publishing.

2. Information We Collect

We collect nothing. CopyUnlock does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal information on any server or third-party service. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, IP address, etc.)
  • No browsing history or visited URLs
  • No clipboard content
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No crash reports sent externally
  • No cookies or tracking pixels
  • No device fingerprinting

3. Local Storage

CopyUnlock stores the following data locally on your device only, using Chrome's built-in storage APIs:

  • Extension settings: Your preferences such as enabled/disabled state, keyboard shortcuts, and Safe Mode configuration.
  • Clipboard history: Copied text items are stored locally for the clipboard manager feature. Free tier retains up to 200 items for 24 hours. Pro tier retains unlimited items indefinitely.
  • Tags and pins: Any organization metadata you create for clipboard items.

This data never leaves your browser. It is not synced across devices unless you explicitly enable Chrome Sync, which is managed by Google, not by us.

4. Network Requests

CopyUnlock makes zero network requests. The extension does not communicate with any server, API, or external service. It operates entirely offline after installation. There is no "phone home" functionality, update checking (handled by Chrome itself), or remote configuration.

5. Permissions

CopyUnlock requests the following Chrome permissions, each with a specific and limited purpose:

  • activeTab: Required to detect and remove copy/paste restrictions on the current page when you activate the extension.
  • clipboardRead / clipboardWrite: Required for the clipboard manager feature to read and write clipboard content.
  • storage: Required to save your preferences and clipboard history locally.

We request only the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function. We do not request broad permissions like "access to all websites" or "read browsing history."

6. Third-Party Services

CopyUnlock does not integrate with, send data to, or receive data from any third-party services. There are no embedded analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), no advertising SDKs, and no social media trackers.

7. Data Sharing

Since we collect no data, there is no data to share. We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise disclose any information to third parties because no information is collected in the first place.

8. Children's Privacy

CopyUnlock does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Clipboard history: You can clear it at any time from the extension popup. Free tier items auto-delete after 24 hours.
  • All data: Uninstalling the extension from Chrome automatically removes all stored data.
  • Manual reset: You can reset the extension to default settings from the options page.

10. Security

Your data is protected by Chrome's built-in extension sandboxing and storage encryption. Each extension's storage is isolated from other extensions and websites. We follow Chrome extension security best practices and our extension code is available for inspection.

11. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify users through the extension's update notes in the Chrome Web Store. Our commitment to zero data collection will not change.

12. Open Source

CopyUnlock's source code is available on GitHub for anyone to audit and verify our privacy claims. We believe transparency is the strongest form of privacy assurance.

13. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or CopyUnlock's data practices, please contact us:

  • Publisher: PeakTools Publishing
  • GitHub: github.com/peaktools/copyunlock

Our promise: We built CopyUnlock because we believe copying text from a webpage shouldn't require giving up your privacy. This extension will always respect your data. Zero collection, zero tracking, zero compromises.
