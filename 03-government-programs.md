## Why Government Won't Work Right Now

1. **SBIR/STTR is literally frozen** -- Congressional authorization expired Sept 30, 2025
2. **GSA Schedule requires $6,000-50,000 upfront** plus 2 years of commercial sales
3. **8(a) requires 2 years operating history** in your primary industry
4. **DOGE has terminated 13,440+ government contracts** -- landscape is hostile
5. **18F was eliminated in March 2025** by DOGE
6. **State/local grants** for individual software devs are essentially nonexistent

---

## What IS Worth Doing (Free)

1. **Register on SAM.gov** -- Free. Government agencies can buy software under $15K without bidding. Just be findable.
2. **Track SBIR reauthorization** -- When Congress acts, apply immediately. Phase I = $150-300K for 6 months of R&D.
3. **Freelance first, government later** -- Build 2 years of revenue, THEN pursue GSA Schedule and 8(a).

## Bug Bounties — Reality Check

- HackerOne paid $81M total last year
- **95% of bug bounty hunters quit**
- Top 5% earn 50% of all bounties
- Beginners: $0-500/mo for 6-12 months while learning
- Average payout: $500-5,000 per validated bug
- Requires web security expertise, not just dev skills
- NOT a reliable income source

---

## Bottom Line

Government programs are designed for companies that ALREADY have money, revenue, and track record. They are NOT designed to help a solo dev with $0-30 get started. The fastest path from $0 to income is freelancing + digital products, not government grants.

**Future play (Year 2+)**: Once Zafto has commercial revenue, SBIR Phase I ($150-300K) for "AI-powered construction project management for government facilities" is a legitimate opportunity.
